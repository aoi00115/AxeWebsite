HISTORY




Axe was launched in France in 1983 by Unilever. It was inspired by
another of Unilever's brands, Impulse. Unilever introduced many
products in the range, but were forced to use the name Lynx in
the United Kingdom, Ireland, Australia,and New Zealand due to
trademark issues with the Axe name. In addition, some countries
(such as South Africa) introduced the brand as EGO. Scents have
evolved over time. From 1983 until about 1989, the variant names were
descriptions of the fragrances and included Musk, Spice, Amber,
Oriental, and Marine. From 1990 until 1996, geographic names for
fragrances were used. In 2009, the brand launched an eight-centimetre
container called the Axe Bullet. The brand has also extended
into other areas.
